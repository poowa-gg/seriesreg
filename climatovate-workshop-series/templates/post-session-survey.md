## Question 2: Ground-Truthing (Climate Impact Data)

### In the past 30 days, which climate events have affected your community? *
- **Type:** Checkboxes
- **Options:**
  - Heavy rainfall/flooding
  - Drought/water scarcity
  - Extreme heat
  - Poor air quality/harmattan dust
  - Unpredictable weather patterns
  - Crop damage due to weather
  - Road/transport disruption from weather
  - Health issues related to weather
  - Fire outbreak
  - None of the above
  - Other (please specify)

### Briefly describe the impact (Optional but encouraged)
- **Type:** Paragraph
- **Helper text:** 
```
Example: "Flooding for my area spoil road for 2 weeks" 
or "Heat wave affect my poultry farm, I lose 50 birds"

Please include your LGA if comfortable sharing.
```
