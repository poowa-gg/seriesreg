# Standardized Slide/Handout Template

## Slide Deck Structure (Google Slides/PowerPoint)

### Brand Guidelines
- **Primary Color:** #1B5E20 (Forest Green)
- **Secondary Color:** #FF6F00 (Warm Orange)
- **Accent Color:** #0277BD (Sky Blue)
- **Font - Headings:** Montserrat Bold
- **Font - Body:** Open Sans Regular
- **Logo Placement:** Top-right corner on all slides
